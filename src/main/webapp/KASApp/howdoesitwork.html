For retailers:


Registration and Payment Options


1. Customer needs to register with the company by providing the required details.

2. An account is created for the customer and a unique User Id and password is provided (password can be changed by the users).

3. Customer can make payment by depositing money physically or by online transfer (Account details provided at the time of registration) in the KAS Global’s bank account.

4. Once the payment is received, the account will be topped up and customer can start selling cards with different denominations.


What the Company provides?


The following Products/ Services are provided by the company.


1. STAR TSP 100 (80 mm Thermal All-in-One Receipt Printer) with 4 years instant replacement included for free. (Note: A minimum deposit for the printer is chargeable)

2. All the cables and accessories included in the box.

3. Initial setup and installation guide.

4. Our customer support team will guide you with installation issues and setting up your account with the company.


What do you need to have?


The retailer needs to have the following in order to start using our service.


1. A (PC) Personal Computer or an EPOS system (Touch Screen system).

2. Internet connection.
